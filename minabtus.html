Welcome to Dems High School.

As the Founder of this outstanding school, I am pleased to offer you further insight into educational life at Dems High School. Our website is here to give you a glimpse into Dems High School, however I warmly invite you to visit us so that you may learn even more.
We firmly believe in providing our pupils with an education that is unmatched in quality. Our pupils and teachers are at the heart of our establishment and this drives us to constantly improve each student’s experience.
Dems High School is renowned for having one of the highest standards for education in Nigeria and we constantly look to modernise our curriculum whenever possible. The success of our pupils is a result of our commitment to fulfil our school motto: “Learning today, leading tomorrow”.
We believe in letting our students grow and this is why we provide state-of-the-art facilities and equipment so that they can achieve their full potential. However, we would not be leaders in the Nigerian education system without the dedication of our experienced teaching faculty and their use of the most effective teaching methods.
Dems High School continues to raise genuine, well-educated, responsible, God-fearing and strong-minded students who go on to achieve amazing things. Our proven record of excellence for education in Nigeria is what has driven so many families to join our esteemed educational institution.
We hope that you will pursue a visit to Dems High School so that your children can be part of the legacy we continue to build on.
