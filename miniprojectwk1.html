A Warm Welcome


We are dedicated to the advancement of humanity, through quality education, dedicated service and total commitment.

The light is our focus, love is our sustenance. In this new age of awareness, an all around education is the answer to all challenges of life. With our light and our love, the best is our lot.

The Day School

Our day school is conveniently located in the heart of Jibowu and is easily accessible by families wishing to send their children daily to learn in Dems High School.
Students have the option to commute to school and access the excellent educational facilities available at the college.
School buses are available to students to ease the daily transportation process.
